Governor Newsom signed new housing laws on October 6, 2025. These changes affect property owners, managers, and tenants across California.

What's Happening

California has implemented several new housing laws that directly impact property management and tenant rights. These laws include:

  • AB 628: Working appliances required in all rental units (effective Jan 1, 2026)
  • AB 246: Protection for tenants with delayed Social Security benefits
  • AB 414: Electronic security deposit returns allowed
  • AB 863: Multilingual eviction forms required (effective Jan 1, 2027)
  • AB 325: Restrictions on pricing algorithms for rent
