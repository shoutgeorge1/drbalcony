Housing Laws Status Update

Current Status: New Housing Laws Implementation

Governor Newsom signed comprehensive housing legislation on October 6, 2025. Implementation deadlines are approaching for property owners and managers.

Implementation Status

AB 628 - Habitability Standards

Status: Effective January 1, 2026

Requires working stoves and refrigerators in all rental units

AB 246 - Social Security Protection

Status: Effective January 1, 2026

Protects tenants from eviction due to delayed Social Security benefits

AB 414 - Electronic Security Deposits

Status: Effective January 1, 2026

Allows electronic return of security deposits with tenant consent

AB 863 - Multilingual Eviction Forms

Status: Effective January 1, 2027

Requires multilingual eviction summons in 6 languages

AB 325 - Pricing Algorithm Restrictions

Status: Effective January 1, 2026

Prohibits use of pricing algorithms for rent collusion

URGENT: SB-721 Apartment Inspections

Deadline: January 1, 2026

Apartment buildings with 3+ units and balconies/decks 6+ feet high must complete inspections

Required Actions

  • Review all rental units for appliance functionality
  • Update lease agreements to reflect new requirements
  • Develop policies for Social Security payment delays
  • Implement electronic security deposit systems
  • Schedule SB-721 inspections if not completed
  • Review pricing software for compliance
