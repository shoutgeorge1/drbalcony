Frequently Asked Questions

What properties require SB-721 or SB-326 inspections?
SB-721 applies to apartments and multifamily rental properties with 3+ units. SB-326 applies to condominiums and HOAs.
How often do inspections need to be performed?
Initial inspections by January 1, 2025. SB-721 requires inspections every 6 years; SB-326 every 9 years.
What does the inspection include?
Comprehensive inspection of all exterior elevated elements including balconies, decks, stairways, and railings.
How much do inspections cost?
Most inspections range from $1,500 to $5,000 depending on property size and complexity.
What happens if issues are found?
We provide a detailed report with prioritized recommendations. You'll have time to plan repairs, and we can connect you with qualified contractors.
How long does the process take?
On-site inspections typically complete in 1-3 days. Comprehensive reports are delivered within 5-7 business days.
